Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CogniStream AI.

How does CogniStream AI work?

Our system uses the Llama 3 architecture powered by Groq's high-speed inference engine. It analyzes your queries and generates structured learning materials including roadmaps, resources, and exercises.

Is the content always accurate?

While our AI models are highly advanced and trained on vast datasets, they can occasionally produce inaccuracies. We recommend cross-referencing critical information with the curated resources provided in the librarian tab.

Can I save my learning progress?

Currently, the public version allows you to generate and view materials. Registered users can save their roadmaps and chat history to their profiles for future reference.

Is it free to use?

Yes, the basic AI learning features are free to use. We aim to keep core educational tools accessible to everyone.

Which subjects are supported?

The AI is capable of teaching almost any academic or technical subject, from quantum physics and advanced mathematics to coding, history, and literature.
